The modern prayer chain

The STEEPULL prayer chain is interactive, increases engagement, and encourages building and solidifying relationships in your church community

Email lists, social media groups and text messages are hard to manage, require a gatekeeper and don't provide a platform for the church member in need of prayer to interact with their church family. STEEPULL allows anyone in your church to post their prayer needs and for their fellow church members to reply and indicate they are covering the request in prayer.

A safe place to share your prayer needs with your church family

STEEPULL allows you to share prayer requests with your church family, other churches in your region or the general public.

Some prayer requests are better kept in house, while others may be pertinent to other churches in your area or even the general public. STEEPULL gives you the flexibility to choose who sees your prayer requests, including sharing your public posts on social media if you desire. STEEPULL provides a platform where everyone in your church community can feel engaged in the prayer needs within your church, but also allows you to provide access to others outside of your church when it is the right fit for your prayer need.

Add prayer requests and other church members to your daily prayer list

STEEPULL allows you to add prayer requests and other church members to a daily prayer list

We believe prayer is powerful, and that includes simply praying for a fellow church member that is on your heart recently. STEEPULL allows you to add both prayer requests and other church members to a prayer list and lets you log your prayers each day. As with all STEEPULL prayer requests, members can allow push notifications to let you know you are praying for them or their prayer requests the moment you pray.

Easily manage users and contacts in one place

STEEPULL allow admin users to easily manage users and contacts from your.

STEEPULL comes with a built in directory for regular users and admin functionality for administrative users. This allows admins to to easily manage users as well as other members of your church that have not started using the app yet. You can all easily create a list of members that have not joined yet and that may need to be called or emailed about a specficic prayer request.
